Welcome to the Portal

Here is a quick guide to logging your shifts and expenses:

  • 1. Setup: Set your Hourly Rate and Target Goal. Toggle "Auto-Tax" if you want 15% automatically deducted from your gross pay.
  • 2. Time Magic: Click "+ Add Shift". Type your times (e.g., "9:03 AM"). The system instantly rounds it to the nearest 10 minutes for you.
  • 3. Expenses: Log any port expenses or deliveries in the Liabilities section. It subtracts directly from your total.
  • 4. Track: Watch your progress bar fill up as you hit your target goal!
